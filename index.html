About Me

My name is Ben Peterson and I am a rising 4th year student at Northeastern University pursuing a Bachelors of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering. I have always been a hands-on learner, which made the Cooperative Education program at Northeastern a key reason why I decided to attend. Through this program I have had the opportunity to learn from two 6-month co-ops in product development.

In 2016 I was a project Engineer on the Kids Gaming team at Hasbro Inc. in Pawtucket, RI, and most recently in 2017, I was a Mechanical Engineer at Continuum Innovation in Boston's Seaport. These experiences have been invaluable towards advancing my skills, applying my education to industry experience, and continuing to help me develop an understanding for what I am most passionate about.

This site is an attempt to capture those passions, share what one could find me up to outside of the classroom, and showcase my interest in exploring the relationshops between engineering and user-centered design. Please feel free to reach out to me with any questions or comments.
